Projects

I tend to host most of my projects on my GitHub page, however, some things are unfortunately private due to their nature, or simply not ready to be released yet. Some of my more notable public projects include:

  • - Ausonia: A Discord Bot, written with the PyCord Framework, that aims to bring AI Services, mainly Image Generation, to Discord.   • - AICLI: A Command Line Inference which intends to provide a simple way of inferencing Image and Text Generation Models from a range of sources, both locally and from online services such as OpenAI.   • - Brain Tumor Detection with ResNet50: A project which explores the use of the ResNet50 CNN to detect four types of Brain Tumors from MRI Images. The Model was trained using PyTorch and the MRI-Images-of-Brain-Tumor dataset from PranomVignesh. In its evaluation, the Model achieved an accuracy of 93%, with an average loss of 0.24 and an average F1 Score of 0.926.
